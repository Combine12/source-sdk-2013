HALLOWEEN HL2: Pumpkin Night REDUX

=========================
INFO
=========================
Pumpkin Night is a singleplayer mod, for Half-life 2, to be released in October 2005. It is not a short version of "Halloween HL2: The Traveler" but a small mod showing a glimpse of the Halloween HL2 story from a different perspective. 
It starts in a similar fashion, a young boy on Halloween night Decides to take a detour into an old farmhouse after an evening of Trick-or-Treating. He stumbles upon the Ostium Mortis, an ancient book. But the prophecy of the traveler was only partially fulfilled, and the false traveler must be punished for his discovery.

3. Open up Steam » Third-Party Games » Start playing!
